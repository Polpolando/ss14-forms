This request must be accompanied by a current and complete Budget Report document (RP-BDG) outlining the financial status of all departments involved.

All budget reallocations require the official stamps of approval from the Heads of the respective departments. Should mutual agreement not be reached, a formal resolution meeting must be held in the Conference Room. This meeting shall include all involved Department Heads, the Captain, and the Head of Personnel. The reallocation shall not be executed until consensus is achieved and all necessary endorsements are obtained.
